☆ about me

hello. i go by nork, a strange individual on the web. im a bit bad at explaining myself but i hope you dont mind.

im a black 19 year old autistic adhd combo queer transmasc who uses any pronoun you can think of. i guess you can say im a bit of an ambivert? i like to be kept to myself sometimes, left alone to my own devices, but very talkative when the opportunity presents itself. im loud, dopey, a slight hot head (i can control it. kind of) but i am totally normal.

i have other hobbies, such as drawing, learning instruments, and exploring the wide web. i have a BIG attachment for horror and have a slight morbid curiosity, almost watching/playing any horror media out there available for me and indulging myself in disturbing mysteries. my strongest media connections is and always saw, silent hill and the five nights at freddy's franchise. all three means a lot to me.

☆ about site

ive been coding for about 3 years by now. during the pandemic, i was bored out of my mind, rotting away in my room while the days went by. out of that bordom, i discovered coding languages. my first coding language was LUA, a big start to coding for me, but it was soley for making games on roblox when i was in my prime roblox fixation. after that fixation slowed down, i went to HTML and CSS sometime around 2022, making profile themes on spacehey. i mostly developed skills through tweaking with F2U templates, like taking a toy apart and putting it back together.

with too much time on my hands, i made nork.neocities.org. a site that was red emo themed, but started to shift around a lot due to my indecisiveness. it was a bit messy trying to find what was "me", until i scrapped nork.neocities.org and made the one i currently have now.

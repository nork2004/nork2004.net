11/16/2023

FIRST BLOG POST! today was pretty ordinary. i stayed in bed napping all day after i did an all-nighter with my messy sleep schedule as usual, then got up to watch more movies and videos i came across on my recommended.

woke up to no one in the house except my sibling, my cat ollie bothering me about food in her plate (when she eats like every minute...), and the dogs napping. so this is pretty much just another plain day going by. kind of getting old.

speaking of my cat, here's the little devil on the left side :-). and when i mean little devil, i mean literally. shes probably the most unfriendly cat i've ever seen or had, but only tolerates me and my sibling (when she wants to), but i love her evil ass bigger than my whole heart, ever since i got her back in 2016.

getting back on topic, the day went by fast like always. it seems like when the winter season approaches, things tend to be more dull than ever (probably because i like autumn better). as december arrives, i hope next year will be something very different for me.

☆ favorite artists

Michael Jackson - 2013

my most favorite ever since i was in elementary. an american singer, songwriter, dancer, and philanthropist - that fundamentally influenced pop culture.

i discovered him through my family (they are mega fans), and instantly fell in love with his WHOLE discography. i personally think he doesn't have ONE bad song.

Björk - 2018

an icelandic singer and musician with a very distinct voice and beautiful style in musical choices and style.

i discovered her in 2018 mostly through my brother who is a pretty big fan of her. her debut album literally called Debut sparked interest in me when i heard "Venus as a Boy". and not only her music but her public presence.

Blur - 2016

an english rock band formed in 1988 and helped define the britpop genre.

i discovered them through Gorillaz back around 2016, since the lead singer, damon albarn, is the singing vocal for Gorillaz's lead singer 2D. i immediately fell in love with their sound, and how they started to experiment later in their band days.

Queen - 2018

an english rock band formed in 1970 and is my most favorite rock 70s bands EVER. even got me more into the classic rock genre.

i discovered them through my classic rock fixation around 2018-2019, and started getting attached to their whole discography and history during the Bohemian Rhapsody biopic era.

Radiohead - 2017

an english rock band formed in 1985 that pretty much carried me in middle school i CANNOT thank them enou-

i discovered them around my britpop fixation in 2016-2017 i think? i was starting to look into more rock bands (mostly from britain loooool british inavsion) and thats where i found them. a very BIG influence they had on me, to the point where i wanted to start guitar lessons myself! and i think that goes for every rock band i like in this section lol...

Gorillaz - 2016

an english virtual band formed in 1998 that inspires me artistically and musically.

i discovered them sometime around 2016-2018 when i first listened to their track called "DARE" from their second album Demon Days. their music, music videos, and story telling pulled me in HARD and stuck with me since middle school.

Incubus - 2018

an american rock banded formed in 1991 that I LOVE SO MUCH AAAAAH. one of my most favorite 90's alt bands.

i discovered them around 2018 when i listened to their most popular track "Drive" for the first time like any other new fan. i started listening to more to their stuff and found out their music was linked to a genre called "nu-metal". basically they helped me discover what nu-metal was and thats why i love it so much till this day.
